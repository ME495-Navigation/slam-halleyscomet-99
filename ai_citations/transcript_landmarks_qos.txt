Topic: ROS 2 QoS incompatibility — real LiDAR data not received by landmarks node

--- Summary of interaction ---

After deploying to the physical TurtleBot3, the landmarks node was not
receiving any data on /scan despite the topic existing and the LiDAR
publishing at ~2.5 Hz.  The terminal showed:

  [landmarks] [WARN]: New publisher discovered on topic '/scan',
  offering incompatible QoS. No messages will be sent to it.
  Last incompatible policy: RELIABILITY_QOS_POLICY

I asked what causes this warning and how to fix it without changing the
LiDAR driver.  The explanation was that the hls_lfcd_lds_driver publishes
with BEST_EFFORT reliability, while the default rclcpp subscription uses
RELIABLE.  These are incompatible under ROS 2 DDS QoS negotiation rules.

The fix was to construct the subscription with an explicit QoS profile:
  const auto qos = rclcpp::QoS(rclcpp::KeepLast(10)).best_effort();

I asked whether this change would break the simulated scan subscriber,
and confirmed it would not because nusimulator publishes with RELIABLE
and a BEST_EFFORT subscriber accepts both policies.
